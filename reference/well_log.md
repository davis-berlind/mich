# Shankle Oil Well Lithology

Six petrophysical measures collected by the Kansas Geological Survey by
drilling into the B1-C formations of the Shankle well in the Panoma oil
and gas field of southwest Kansas.

## Usage

``` r
well_log
```

## Format

### `well_log`

A data frame with 346 rows and 8 columns:

- Facies:

  1: "Nonmarine Sandstone, 2: "Nonmarine Coarse Siltstone, 3: Nonmarine
  Fine Siltstone, 4: Marine Siltstone/Shale, 5: Mudstone, 6: Wackestone,
  7: Dolomite, 8: Packstone-Grainstone,8: Packstone-Grainstone"

- Depth:

  Well depth in feet

- GR:

  Gamma ray emissions

- Rt:

  Deep resistivity

- DeltaPhi:

  Difference of neutron and density porosity

- AvgPhi:

  Average neutron and density porosity

- PE:

  Photoelectric factor

- MnM:

  Marine/non-marine indicator

## Source

Bohling, G. and M. Dubois (2003). An integrated application of neural
network and markov chain techniques to the prediction of lithofacies
from well logs: Kansas geological survey open-file report 2003-50, 6 p.
Group 6.
